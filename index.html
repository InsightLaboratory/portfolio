Who am I?

I love helping people solve problems

I have a passion for solving problems, whether it be real-world problems for industry using user experience (UX), tackling code bugs in websites or teaching students how to solve problems with design and UX research. When I started studying web design and development back in 2000 I was inspired by my TAFE college teachers. I decided right then I wanted to combine my two passions — creating websites and teaching. Now I’m lucky enough to do both as a lecturer at Swinburne University of Technology.
